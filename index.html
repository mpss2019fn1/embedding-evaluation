A Simple Framework to Mine Insights from Natural Language Model¶

We want to support the process of interpreting learned characteristics of Natural Language Embeddings. We aim to shed light on covered and missed semantics using a knowledge base.

Another key contribution is it to map dimensions to semantics or facts.
